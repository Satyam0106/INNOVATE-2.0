Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I submit a complaint?

You can submit a complaint by selecting a category on the homepage and filling out the feedback form.

2. What categories can I choose from?

We have categories such as Electricity, Water, Garbage, Roads, and Buildings.

3.⁠⁠How long does it take for a complaint to be resolved?

The time for resolution varies depending on the nature of the complaint. Most complaints are acknowledged within 48 hours, and you can track progress online.

4. ⁠Will my personal information be kept confidential?

Yes, your personal information is confidential and only used for resolving your complaint. The portal follows strict data protection guidelines.

5. Who can I contact for further assistance?

You can contact our support team via the contact page for any further assistance.
